Features

Everything in one place

No more juggling three apps. Forge handles your workouts, food, and progress in a single clean interface.

🏋️
Workout Tracking
Log every set, rep, and weight. Forge tracks progressive overload automatically so you always know when to push harder.
🥗
Nutrition Logging
Barcode scanner, food search, and meal templates. Hit your calorie and macro targets without the mental overhead.
✨
AI Food Analysis
Snap a photo of your meal and let AI estimate the macros. No more manual lookups for every ingredient.
📈
Progress Charts
Interactive charts for body weight, strength PRs, and volume over time. See the trend, stay motivated.
🔔
Smart Reminders
Schedule daily workout reminders at the time that works for you. Never skip another session.
⚡
Offline First
Log workouts and meals even without internet. Everything syncs automatically when you're back online.

How it works

Simple from day one

Step 03

Log your workouts and meals. Let Forge show you the patterns — and where you're improving.
